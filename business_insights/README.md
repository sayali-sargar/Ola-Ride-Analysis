# Business Insights from Ola Ride Analysis

Based on the SQL analysis and Power BI dashboard, the following key insights were identified from the Ola ride booking data.

## Key Insights

- A large proportion of ride bookings were successfully completed, indicating consistent demand for Ola services.
- Ride cancellations occurred from both customers and drivers, highlighting potential operational and coordination challenges.
- Certain vehicle types contributed more significantly to overall booking value, making them important from a revenue perspective.
- Customer and driver ratings provided useful signals about service quality and user experience.
- Analysis of ride distance and booking patterns helped identify peak usage behavior and high-demand scenarios.

## Business Takeaways

- Improving coordination between drivers and customers could help reduce cancellations.
- High-performing vehicle categories can be prioritized for better availability during peak hours.
- Ratings data can be leveraged to improve service quality and driver performance.
